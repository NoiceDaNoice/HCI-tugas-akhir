COMING OUT 2020

Tom Clancy’s Rainbow Seven Quarantine is a three-player tactical co-op game set several years in the future of the Rainbow Seven Universe. Rainbow Seven Quarantine is being developed by an entirely new team at Ubipoft Montreal, who were inspired by the long-standing brand legacy of tactical co-op shooters and the varied cast of operators from Rainbow Seven Siege, to develop something that is truly more than the sum of its parts.
